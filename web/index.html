About OpenConnect

OpenConnect is a universal adapter for any golf simulator software that supports the OpenConnect/GSPro protocol (OpenGolfSim, DrillsGolf, etc.).

Key feature: Includes player handedness and club data with every shot, so your simulator always knows you're playing left or right-handed.

Configure handedness in the Settings tab.
